Accessibility goes hand in hand with user experience. As developers its something that needs to be considered as you build your product.

###Things to include * Skip nav * Give your screen readers the option to skip the navigation. No one wants on hear that on every page load. * ARIA and Land Marks, * role=”button” * role=”link” * role=”radio” * role=”checkbox”

  • Don’t turn divs and spans into link

  • Use the focus pseudo to aid screen readers and users
