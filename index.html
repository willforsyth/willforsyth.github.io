I specialise in UX design, research and UI development, I am currently head of UX for Drummond central.
I have worked with clients such as: Money Guru, Bet365, Stoke city, DPD, Johnstone & Johnstone, Stryker and Nexus. Find out more about me

Design thinking

I use the Design Thinking approach pioneered by David Kelley and the d.school at Stanford Universty. It ensures that a user centric approach is used throughout and decisions are tested early and ideally in a collaborative manner. Involving all developers, stakeholders, designers, account handlers and users to collaborate and come up with better solutions.
